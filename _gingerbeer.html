The Story Behind Our Name

Our name "The Minty Cat" comes from a delightful play on words rooted in Malagasy culture.

In the Malagasy language, ginger is called "Sakamalao" - a word that can be beautifully split into two parts:

"Saka"

means Cat

"Malao"

means Minty

And so, "The Minty Cat" was born - celebrating both our local heritage and the fresh, invigorating taste of our artisanal ginger beer!

Our Two Signature Versions

Classic

The Classic Version

Sweeter & Milder

Perfect for those who enjoy a gentler introduction to ginger beer. Our classic version offers a smooth, balanced taste with just the right amount of ginger warmth, complemented by natural sweetness and a hint of lemon freshness.

  • Smooth and balanced flavor
  • Perfect for all ages
  • Refreshing any time of day
Intense

The Intense Version

Stronger & Less Sweet

For the bold and adventurous! Our intense version packs a powerful ginger punch with reduced sweetness, allowing the natural spiciness and complexity of fresh ginger to take center stage. A true ginger lover's delight.

  • Bold ginger flavor
  • Lower sugar content
  • Perfect digestive aid
