The Story Behind the Name

Our brand name "The Minty Cat" comes from a delightful pun in the Malagasy language. In Malagasy, ginger is called "Sakamalao" which can be cleverly split into two parts:

  • "Saka" = Cat
  • "Malao" = Minty

Hence, The Minty Cat - a name that celebrates both our local heritage and the refreshing essence of our ginger beer.

Pure & Natural Ingredients

At The Minty Cat, we believe in keeping things simple and natural. Our ginger beer is brewed using only four pure ingredients: water, sugar, fresh ginger and lemon.

No colorants, no artificial sweeteners, and no preservatives

Every bottle is a testament to traditional brewing methods combined with our passion for authentic and refreshing taste.

Two Distinct Versions

Classic Version

Milde and sweet, perfect for those who prefer a gentle introduction to ginger beer. Smooth and refreshing with a balanced ginger kick.

Composition:

  • Water: 64%
  • Sugar: 31%
  • Ginger: 4%
  • Lemon: <1%

Intense Version

Strong and less sweet, designed for ginger enthusiasts who crave that authentic and fiery ginger experience. Bold and invigorating.

Composition:

  • Water: 77%
  • Sugar: 15%
  • Ginger: 7%
  • Lemon: <1%
